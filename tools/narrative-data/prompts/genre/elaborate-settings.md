You are helping build a multidimensional setting model for a narrative engine. Your task is to describe how the setting **{target_name}** is specifically shaped by its genre context.

## Important: Focus on the Delta

You have access to both the setting's standalone description and the genre's dimensional description in the Additional Context sections below. **Do not repeat the standalone setting description.** Your job is to describe what changes — what this specific genre does to this setting that makes its expression here distinct from the genre-agnostic baseline.

Think of the standalone description as the setting at rest. This prompt asks: what happens when you place it in this genre's gravitational field?

## Setting Dimension Shifts

For each setting dimension, describe the direction and magnitude of the shift this genre imposes. Not every dimension will shift — identify the ones that do and explain why.

- **Atmospheric signature** — does this genre push the setting toward greater menace or greater comfort? Why? What about the genre's tonal register and thematic preoccupations creates this pressure? Does the mood become more oppressive, more welcoming, more sublime, or more unsettling?
- **Sensory dominance** — does the genre change which senses this setting privileges? Does the genre push toward visual beauty, auditory dread, tactile discomfort, or olfactory memory? What about the genre's aesthetic register determines the sensory hierarchy?
- **Temporal character** — does the genre change how time feels in this setting? Does it push toward timelessness, urgency, decay, or cyclical return? What about the genre's temporal orientation creates this shift?
- **Spatial orientation** — does the genre change the felt scale of this setting? Does it push toward claustrophobia or agoraphobia, containment or exposure? What about the genre's structural shape and agency model determines spatial experience?
- **Narrative permeability** — does the genre seal this setting off or open it to the outside world? Can characters leave freely, or does the genre impose confinement? What about the genre's structural shape determines permeability?
- **Symbolic density** — does the genre increase or decrease how much symbolic weight this setting carries? Does the genre demand that the setting mean something beyond its physical reality? What about the genre's thematic commitments determines symbolic load?
- **Communicability** — does the genre push the setting toward active participation or passive containment? Does the setting become an agent in this genre — conferring, withholding, corrupting, nurturing — or does it recede into backdrop?

For each shift, be specific: "Atmospheric Signature darkens from neutral to threatening because..." is more useful than "Atmospheric Signature changes."

## Affordance Effects

How do this genre's specific dimensional properties reshape the setting?

- **Locus of power** — where power resides in this genre determines the setting's narrative role. If power is in place, this setting may confer or withhold power. If power is in knowledge, the setting becomes a container for information — what can be discovered here? If power is in relationships, the setting shapes how characters relate by controlling proximity, privacy, and visibility. Describe the specific interaction.
- **Temporal orientation** — does this genre's relationship to time change the setting's temporal character? In cyclical genres, the setting may be a place of return — characters come back and find it changed or unchanged. In linear genres, the setting may be encountered once and left behind. Does the setting accumulate history or exist in an eternal present?
- **State variables** — which of the genre's tracked resources does this setting interact with? Does the setting deplete safety, accumulate symbolic weight, erode sanity, build community, or spend isolation? How does the setting's presence on the narrative stage affect the genre's resource economy?
- **World affordances** — what does this genre's physics (magic, technology, violence consequences, death permanence, supernatural presence) do to the setting's material reality? Can the setting do things in this genre that it cannot do elsewhere? Does the genre's physics change what this setting is made of, how it behaves, or what it contains?

## Excluded Expressions

What versions of this setting are structurally forbidden by this genre? Every genre's exclusion list constrains the space of possible spatial-atmospheric expressions. If the genre rejects safety, this setting cannot function as a refuge — it must be subverted. If the genre rejects beauty, the setting's aesthetic potential is constrained.

Be specific about what is excluded and why. This is as informative as what is permitted — it defines the setting's shape by carving away what it cannot be in this context.

## Genre-Specific Sensory Palette

How does this setting's sensory experience change in this genre?

- What specific sensory details become prominent or suppressed because of this genre's aesthetic register?
- What does the genre's characteristic light quality, soundscape, and texture vocabulary do to this setting's sensory identity?
- What emotional valence do sensory details carry in this genre that they do not carry elsewhere? (A crackling fire means safety in cozy fantasy and destruction in dark fantasy — what genre-specific emotional coding applies to this setting's sensory details?)

## Genre-Specific Communicability

How do the setting's communicability dimensions change in this genre?

- **Atmospheric** — how strongly does this setting broadcast mood in this genre? Does the genre amplify or mute the setting's atmospheric signal?
- **Sensory** — what narratively relevant information does this setting provide through sensory channels that is specific to this genre's concerns?
- **Spatial** — how does this genre's relationship to space change what the setting's geometry communicates about power, safety, and freedom?
- **Temporal** — how does this genre's relationship to time change what the setting communicates about urgency, permanence, and change?

## Distinctive Genre Expression

Finally, describe what is uniquely true about this setting in this genre that is not true elsewhere. What atmospheric qualities, sensory textures, narrative functions, or communicability profiles exist only at this intersection of setting and genre? This is the most valuable output — the genre-specific spatial knowledge that cannot be derived from either the standalone setting description or the genre description alone.

Be expansive. This is exploratory research, not a database entry.
